Working Group on Model-Based Clustering Summer Session: Nice, July 21-25, 2025

The 32nd Summer Working Group on Model-Based Clustering will be held at Université Côte d’Azur, Nice (France), from July 21 to July 25, 2025.

The scientific committee consists this year of Luca Scrucca, Charles Bouveyron, Gilles Celeux, Bettina Grün, Brendan Murphy, Rebecca Nugent, Adrian Raftery, Cinzia Viroli, Pierre-Alexandre Mattei, Marco Corneli, and Vincent Vandewalle.

Venue, Accommodations, and Social activities

The WGMBC will be hosted at the Inria Centre at Université Côte d’Azur (Sophia Antipolis, France). Sophia Antipolis is a large tech park located in a forest in the French Riviera, near the cities of Nice, Antibes, and Cannes. For details on how to reach the Inria campus, see here

  • Ice breaker: on Sunday evening, around 7pm (happy hour :-)) this is the occasion to meet old and new colleagues. This year we plan to do in on a nice beach in Antibes

  • Social lunch: on Tuesday, at noon, it will be here (around 30 euros per person). It is just by the side of the INRIA center where the WG will be located. Ten minutes walking.

  • Social dinner: on Thursday evening, once more in Antibes, we’ll have dinner at the legendary Bistrot Margot (around 40 euros per person). A vegetarian menù can be put in place if needed.

  • Hike: on Saturday, this year we propose a hike on the mountain, about 1h30 north of Nice, possibly with some nice lake of altitude and/or a beautiful view of the sea in the distance. The exact location has not yet been decided, but we have a couple of options that fit with the description.
