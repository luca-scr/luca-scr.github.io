Gaussian Mixture Models for Model-Based Clustering, Classification, and Density Estimation

PhD course

Dottorato in Matematica Informatica e Statistica (UniFI, UniPG, INdAM)
Dottorato in Economia (UniPG)

Contents

  • Statistical learning: supervised vs unsupervised
  • Finite mixture models
  • Gaussian mixture models
  • Model‐based clustering
  • The R package mclust
  • EM algorithm
  • Model selection
  • Density estimation via finite mixture modelling
  • Classification using Gaussian mixture models
  • GMM-based estimation of entropy
  • Anomaly detection: noise and outliers
  • Variable selection for model-based clustering
