Working Group on Model-Based Clustering Summer Session: Perugia, July 16-22, 2017

The 24th Summer Working Group on Model-Based Clustering will be held in Perugia (Italy) from July 16 to July 22, 2017. It will start with an opening social event on Sunday July 16, sessions in the mornings Monday-Friday July 17-21, and a hike on Saturday July 22.

The scientific committee this year consists of Charles Bouveyron, Gilles Celeux, Brendan Murphy, Adrian Raftery and Luca Scrucca.

Venue

WG sessions will be held at the Dipartimento di Giurisprudenza (room #7) of the Università degli Studi di Perugia, and accomodation will be available close to the campus. Accommodation and travel details are given below.

How to reach Perugia

  • By plane: the nearest airport is Perugia S. Egidio, 10km from the city center, but it provides limited flights (www.airport.umbria.it). Other well served international airports are Rome “Fiumicino (Leonardo da Vinci)” and “Ciampino” (2 hours by car, see www.adr.it), Pisa “Galileo Galilei” (3 hours by car, see www.pisa-airport.com), Florence Peretola (2 hours by car, see www.aeroporto.firenze.it) and Ancona “Raffaello Sanzio” (2 hours by car, see www.aeroportomarche.it).
    The easiest choice is Rome “Fiumicino (Leonardo da Vinci)”.
  • By train: from Rome Fiumicino airport there is a direct connection with Termini railway station and it takes about 30 minutes. Then, from Termini railway station several trains are available to reach Perugia. Time table query available at http://www.trenitalia.com/tcom-en.
  • By bus: from Rome Fiumicino airport a bus connection is available to Perugia. Time table available for Rome Fiumicino - Perugia and Perugia - Rome Fiumicino (sorry, only in italian but should be understandable).
Other information can be found at Umbria and Perugia: Territory, Maps and Directions.
