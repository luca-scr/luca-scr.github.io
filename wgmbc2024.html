Working Group on Model-Based Clustering Summer Session: Bertinoro, July 22-26, 2024

The 30th Summer Working Group on Model-Based Clustering will be held in Bertinoro (Italy), from July 22 to July 26, 2024.

The scientific committee consists this year of Luca Scrucca, Charles Bouveyron, Gilles Celeux, Bettina Grün, Brendan Murphy, Rebecca Nugent, Adrian Raftery, Angela Montanari, Cinzia Viroli, and Laura Anderlucci.
