Working Group on Model-Based Clustering Summer Session: Perugia, July 16-22, 2017


The 24th Summer Working Group on Model-Based Clustering will be held in Università degli Studi di Perugia, Italy, from July 16-22, 2017.

How to reach Perugia
  • By plane ...
    The nearest airport is Perugia S. Egidio, 10km from the city center, but it provides limited flights (www.airport.umbria.it).
    Other well served international airports are Rome “Fiumicino (Leonardo da Vinci)” and “Ciampino” (2 hours by car, see www.adr.it), Pisa “Galileo Galilei” (3 hours by car, see www.pisa-airport.com), Florence Peretola (2 hours by car, see www.aeroporto.firenze.it) and Ancona “Raffaello Sanzio” (2 hours by car, see www.aeroportomarche.it).
    The easiest choice is Rome “Fiumicino (Leonardo da Vinci)”.
  • By train ...
    From Rome Fiumicino airport there is a direct connection with Termini railway station and it takes about 30 minutes. Then, from Termini railway station several trains are available to reach Perugia. Time table query available at http://www.trenitalia.com/tcom-en.
  • By bus ...
    From Rome Fiumicino airport a bus connection is available to Perugia. Time table available for Rome Fiumicino - Perugia and Perugia - Rome Fiumicino (sorry, only in italian but should be understandable).
Other information can be found at Umbria and Perugia: Territory, Maps and Directions.
