Working Group on Model-Based Clustering Summer Session: Online meeting, July 25–26, 2022

The 28th Summer Working Group on Model-Based Clustering will be held remotely via Zoom on July 25–26, 2022.

The scientific committee consists this year of Charles Bouveyron, Gilles Celeux, Bettina Grün, Brendan Murphy, Rebecca Nugent, Adrian Raftery and Luca Scrucca.
